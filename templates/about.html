About D&H Meats

Family-owned surplus protein wholesale in Palo, Iowa.

How We Work

We buy surplus from the big packers. When they have product to move, we get the call. That means quality protein — beef, pork, poultry, seafood, dairy — at prices regular distribution can't touch.

D&H Meats operates out of Palo, Iowa, serving restaurants, grocery stores, convenience stores, meat markets, and institutions across the greater Midwest. We're a secondary wholesaler: we work with Smithfield, Tyson, Cargill, and others to bring you competitive pricing on the cuts and products you need.

No corporate layers. No complicated ordering systems. You call Herb, tell him what you need, and he makes it happen. If we have a surplus deal that fits your operation, you'll hear about it first.
